## What are Trophy Models (TMs)?

TMs are interactive 3D digital trophies that represent achievements in a visual, memorable, and verifiable way. Each TM is fully interactive — recipients can rotate, zoom, and explore every detail of the model directly in the browser, on any device.

Unlike a PDF or a static image, a TM turns recognition into an experience people actually want to share.

<Tip>
TMs are **free on every plan**. Create, customize, or import as many models as you want at no additional cost.
</Tip>

## Editor capabilities

The Essential TM editor offers advanced customization with real-time preview:

### Geometry
- **Extrusion depth** (0 to 3 units)
- Configurable **bevel**: size and thickness
- Per-layer or per-path group adjustments

### Materials (3 modes)

| Mode | Description |
|------|-------------|
| **Realistic** | Full PBR: solid or gradient color, roughness, metallic, clearcoat, and iridescence |
| **Glass** | Full transmission with refraction (IOR 1.5). Crystal-clear effect |
| **Prism** | Chromatic dispersion, variable thickness, and anisotropic blur. Rainbow effect |

### Textures
15+ built-in presets: fabric, leather, light/dark marble, gold, silver, copper, holographic, wood, and more. Each texture is adjustable in intensity, scale, and tint — and can be animated.

### Lighting and effects
- HDR environments with configurable intensity
- Contact shadows (position, opacity, blur)
- Bloom/neon (low, medium, high)

### Animations
5 animation presets for export and display:

| Preset | Effect |
|--------|--------|
| None | Static, interactive orbit only |
| Forward rotate | Continuous spin (vertical or horizontal axis) |
| Ease spin | Rotation with easing |
| Bounce | Oscillation with squash/stretch |
| Breath | Scale pulse, no rotation |

### Circular text
Add curved text around the trophy with custom font, spacing, inclination, and independent material settings.

## Drafts

The editor automatically saves your work on every change (auto-save with 800ms debounce). Drafts remain available for **24 hours** and are listed on the editor page, sorted most recent first.

When a trophy is published, the draft is finalized automatically.

## Export formats

| Format | Use |
|--------|-----|
| **GLB** | Complete 3D model with embedded materials, textures, and animations |
| **USDZ** | Augmented reality on iPhone and Mac (AR Quick Look) |
| **Video** | MP4/WebM of the animated trophy — ideal for social media |
| **Thumbnail** | Static PNG for previews and cards |

## 3D Viewer

Trophies are rendered via WebGL with Three.js directly in the browser. The experience is smooth on desktop, tablet, and mobile.

The public viewer (`/b/[badgeId]`) supports customization parameters via URL:
- Background: badge color, transparent, or custom color
- Animation: any preset or disabled
- Watermark: full, mark, or off
- Fullscreen mode

<Tip>
For the best viewing experience, we recommend up-to-date browsers (Chrome, Safari, Firefox, Edge).
</Tip>
