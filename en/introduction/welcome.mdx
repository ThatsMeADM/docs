That's Me is the **world's first RewardTech**. We turn certificates into data, opportunities, and growth — for issuers and achievers alike.

<Info>
**Anti-fraud (SHA-256)** · **Permanent record** · **Next-step AI** · **3D trophies** · **Public API**
</Info>

Every achievement recorded on the platform is **permanent**, **verifiable**, and **shareable**. For achievers, it proves a trajectory. For issuers, it creates an intelligence layer that turns certificates into engagement, data, and growth.
