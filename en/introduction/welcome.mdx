That's Me is the **world's first RewardTech**. We turn certificates into data, paths, and growth — for those who issue and those who achieve.

<Info>
**SHA-256 verification** · **1-click LinkedIn** · **AI recommendations** · **3D trophies** · **Public API**
</Info>

Every achievement recorded on the platform is **permanent**, **verifiable**, and **shareable**. For achievers, it's proof of a journey. For issuers, it's an intelligence layer that turns certificates into engagement, data, and growth.

## Core concepts

| Concept | Description |
|---|---|
| **Event** | A course, competition, or activity that generates certificates |
| **Badge (TM)** | An interactive 3D trophy that gives each achievement its visual form |
| **Certificate** | An issued credential linking a recipient to an event and a badge |
| **Profile** | A recipient's public page with all their achievements |
| **Trail** | A sequence of connected events (e.g. English Level 1 → 2 → 3) |
| **Verified issuer** | An organization verified via KYB — checkmark on all certificates |
