## Getting Started

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What is That's Me?">
    That's Me is the **world's first RewardTech**. We turn certificates into data, paths, and growth — for those who issue and those who achieve.

    Every achievement recorded on the platform takes visual form as an interactive 3D trophy, is secured by a SHA-256 hash, and lives permanently on the recipient's profile. The platform analyzes achievements to recommend next steps and connect people to new opportunities.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Who is it for?">
    **For achievers** — students, athletes, employees, and participants create a free account and build a track record of verifiable, shareable achievements on their [Achievement Wall](/en/features/achievement-wall).

    **For issuers** — companies, schools, organizers, and institutions subscribe to a plan and issue digital achievements with traceability, verification, and 3D trophies. The platform serves four verticals: Corporate & HR, Education & Training, Sports & Competitions, and Sociocultural.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Is the platform free?">
    **For recipients**, yes — creating an account and accepting achievements is completely free.

    Issuers subscribe to paid plans starting at $29/month (Starter, with 200 emissions). There's a Free plan to explore the platform without issuing. See [Emissions & Quota](/en/for-issuers/emissions-and-quota) for details.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How are achievements recorded?">
    The issuer [creates an event](/en/for-issuers/create-event), adds participants, and [issues achievements](/en/for-issuers/issue-and-track). Each certificate receives a unique, immutable SHA-256 hash. The recipient is notified by email (and optionally WhatsApp), accepts the achievement, and it becomes part of their profile.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Is there a mobile app?">
    Not yet in app stores, but the experience is fully optimized for any device. Go to [app.thatsme.com.br](https://app.thatsme.com.br) and add the shortcut to your home screen.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## For Recipients

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How do I receive an achievement?">
    You get a notification by **email** (and optionally **WhatsApp**) with a direct link. Click it, review the details, and choose **Accept** or **Decline**. Once accepted, the achievement appears on your [Achievement Wall](/en/features/achievement-wall) and, if public, in the [social feed](/en/features/social-ecosystem).

    If you don't have an account yet, you'll be invited to create one — your email is auto-filled.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How do I share an achievement?">
    Each achievement has sharing buttons for **LinkedIn**, **WhatsApp**, **X (Twitter)**, **Facebook**, **email**, and **copy link**. On LinkedIn, you can add the achievement directly to your profile's certifications section.

    You can also share the [public certificate page](/en/features/certificate-page) (`thatsme.com.br/c/[id]`) — accessible without login and verifiable by anyone.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How does the Achievement Wall work?">
    The wall is your profile on the platform. It displays all your achievements in a grid organized by year, with search by name, company, category, or username. You can filter, sort (newest, oldest, A–Z, Z–A), and control the visibility of each achievement individually.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How do I hide an achievement?">
    From the **"..."** menu on the achievement card, select **"Make private"**. Private achievements are visible only to you (or anyone with the direct link). This setting is independent of your profile privacy — you can have a public profile and still hide specific achievements.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="I didn't receive the achievement email. What should I do?">
    Check your spam folder. The achievement is linked to the email the issuer used during issuance — confirm with them which address was used. If the email is correct and the achievement doesn't appear, check the **Notifications** tab inside the platform or contact support.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How do I verify a certificate's authenticity?">
    Every achievement includes a **SHA-256 hash** and a **QR code** on its [certificate page](/en/features/certificate-page). Anyone can verify authenticity at `thatsme.com.br/certificate/verify/[hash]`, without needing to log in.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## For Issuers

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How do I start issuing achievements?">
    1. Create an issuer account at [app.thatsme.com.br](https://app.thatsme.com.br)
    2. Subscribe to a plan (Starter, Pro, Business, or Enterprise)
    3. [Create a TM](/en/for-issuers/badge-creator) (3D trophy) — free on every plan
    4. [Create an event](/en/for-issuers/create-event) with a template, details, and dates
    5. [Issue achievements](/en/for-issuers/issue-and-track) by adding participants by name and email, or via CSV
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How much does it cost to issue?">
    Billing is per **emission** (each certificate sent = 1 emission). Plans are:

    | Plan | Emissions/month | Monthly price |
    |------|----------------|---------------|
    | **Free** | 0 | $0 |
    | **Starter** | 200 | $29 |
    | **Pro** | 1,000 | $129 |
    | **Business** | 5,000 | $449 |
    | **Enterprise** | Negotiated | Custom |

    The annual plan offers **2 months free** (17% discount). TMs are free on every plan.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What happens if I exceed my quota?">
    **Overage** allows you to keep issuing beyond the monthly quota. Each extra emission is charged on the next invoice (starting at $0.05/emission on Starter). You can disable overage or set a monthly spending cap in **Settings → Billing → Overage**. Details at [Emissions & Quota](/en/for-issuers/emissions-and-quota).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How do I track issuance status?">
    In the **Emissions Panel** for each event, you can see each certificate's status: **Accepted** (recipient accepted), **Pending** (awaiting response), or **Declined** (recipient rejected). Filter by email and track in real time.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I add other administrators?">
    Yes. Under **account menu → Administrators**, invite team members by email. Limits depend on the plan: Free and Starter (1), Pro (3), Business (10), Enterprise (unlimited). All administrator actions are recorded in auditable logs. See [Administrators](/en/for-issuers/administrators).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How does the API integration work?">
    That's Me offers a public REST API to integrate issuance into your system. See the full documentation at [API](/en/api/v1/overview). We also offer [MCP](/en/mcp/issuer-mcp) servers for issuance via AI agents.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## 3D Trophies (TMs)

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What are TMs?">
    TMs (Trophy Models) are interactive 3D digital trophies that represent achievements in a visual, memorable way. Each TM is fully interactive — recipients can rotate, zoom, and explore the model directly in the browser. They're automatically exported as GLB, USDZ (augmented reality), video (MP4/WebM), and thumbnail PNG. Details at [3D Trophies](/en/introduction/3d-trophies).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How do I create a TM?">
    Three paths are available in the [Badge Creator](/en/for-issuers/badge-creator):

    - **Essential TM** — self-service editor with real-time 3D preview. Upload an image and customize geometry, materials (realistic, glass, or prism), 15+ textures, animations, and lighting. Delivery within 2 business days.
    - **Exclusive TM** — guided brief for the design team to build from scratch. First version within 5 business days, with 2 revision rounds.
    - **Import Model** — upload your own 3D file (.GLB, .FBX). Integration in 2-3 business days.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Do TMs cost anything?">
    No. TMs are **free on every plan**. Create, customize, or import as many as you want at no additional cost. Billing applies only to certificate emissions.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Where are my finished TMs?">
    In the [Badge Gallery](/en/for-issuers/badge-gallery). After completion, the TM appears as pending in the gallery. Once you approve it (status **Accepted**), it becomes available for use in events. The gallery lets you search, sort, edit metadata, and view in fullscreen.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Account, Privacy & Security

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Are my achievements secure?">
    Yes. Each certificate receives a **SHA-256 hash** generated from the issuance data (`post_id | event_id | badge_id | profile_id | user_id | created_at | kyb_verification_id`). The hash is immutable — even if the issuer loses verification later, certificates issued during the verified period retain the proof in the hash. Details at [Verification & Security](/en/for-issuers/verification).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What is KYB verification?">
    KYB (Know Your Business) is the issuer verification process. Two levels:

    - **BASIC** — domain verification via DNS TXT record. Immediate.
    - **VERIFIED** — official company registration document submission. Review in 1-3 business days. Displays a verification badge on the profile and all certificates.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Is my profile public?">
    By default, yes. But you can switch it to **private** in **Settings > Privacy**. In private mode, only you and your followers see your wall. Company profiles are always public.

    Additionally, each achievement can be set to public or private individually — regardless of the profile setting.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Does the platform share my data?">
    No. That's Me is fully compliant with Brazil's **LGPD** (General Data Protection Law). No personal information is disclosed without explicit consent. Achievements only become public if the member opts in. Data can be deleted upon request.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How do I delete my account?">
    Go to **Settings > Account** and request deletion. After two-step verification, all your data will be permanently and securely erased in compliance with the LGPD.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
