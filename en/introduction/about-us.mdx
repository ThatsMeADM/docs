## Who we are

That's Me is the **world's first RewardTech**. We created a new technology category that turns certificates into data, paths, and growth — for those who issue and those who achieve.

Every achievement recorded on the platform takes visual form as an interactive 3D trophy, is secured by SHA-256 cryptography, and lives permanently on the recipient's profile. Over time, these achievements build a verified timeline that reveals patterns, affinities, and opportunities.

## How it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Issuer creates an event">
    Companies, schools, organizers, and institutions register the event on the platform with context, dates, and location. They choose a 3D trophy (TM) that will give the achievement its visual form. The AI automatically extracts skills, level, and keywords from the description.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Certificates are issued">
    The issuer adds recipients (by name and email, or via CSV upload) and issues the achievements. Each certificate receives a unique, immutable SHA-256 hash.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Recipient receives and accepts">
    The recipient gets a notification (email or WhatsApp), views the 3D trophy, and accepts the achievement. It becomes part of their public profile.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sharing and visibility">
    The achievement can be shared on LinkedIn, WhatsApp, X, Pinterest, email, and more — with a verifiable public link and trophy video.
  </Step>
  <Step title="AI recommends the next step">
    The platform analyzes the achievement context and recommends the next relevant event, course, or competition. The path lights up automatically — driving engagement for the achiever and upsell for the issuer.
  </Step>
</Steps>
