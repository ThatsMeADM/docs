## Who we are

That's Me is the **world's first RewardTech**. We created a technology category that turns achievements into data, opportunities, and growth — for achievers and issuers alike.

We connect verifiable achievements to a digital ecosystem that combines registration, visualization, sharing, and progression.

Every achievement recorded on the platform gets a 3D visual representation, is protected by cryptography, and lives on the user's profile. Over time, these achievements form a history capable of revealing patterns, skills, and new possibilities.

## How it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="The issuer creates an event">
    Companies, schools, organizers, and institutions register the event on the platform with context, dates, and location. Then, they define the 3D trophy that will visually represent the achievement.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Achievements are issued">
    The issuer adds participants by name and email or via CSV and carries out the issuance. Each achievement receives a unique identifier protected by cryptography.
  </Step>
  <Step title="The participant receives and accepts">
    The recipient gets a notification by email or WhatsApp, views the 3D trophy, and accepts the achievement. From that moment on, it becomes part of their profile.
  </Step>
  <Step title="The achievement can be shared">
    The record can be shared on social networks, by email, and on WhatsApp, with a verifiable link and its own visual presentation.
  </Step>
  <Step title="The platform amplifies the achievement's value">
    Beyond the record itself, the achievement can serve as the basis for recommendations, discovery of new opportunities, and strengthening the relationship between issuer and participant.
  </Step>
</Steps>
