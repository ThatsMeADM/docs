## LinkedIn Integration

That's Me lets you turn achievements received on the platform into digital certificates that can be added to LinkedIn.

This way, participants from different backgrounds — such as sports, education, corporate, and socio-cultural initiatives — can showcase these achievements on their professional profile with more context and verification.

## How it works

When you open a received achievement, you will see the blue **"In - Add to Profile"** button in the bottom-right corner.

When you click that button, you are redirected to LinkedIn's **"Add license or certificate"** screen, with the fields already filled in, such as:

- Achievement name
- Issuing organization
- Issue date
- Verification code and link

Then, simply click **Save** to add the achievement to your LinkedIn profile.

## How to link your LinkedIn page to That's Me

If you are an issuer and want the achievements you issue to appear linked to your company on LinkedIn:

1. Go to your corporate LinkedIn page using a profile that is an **administrator** of the page.

   Example URL:

   `https://www.linkedin.com/company/105616287/admin/dashboard/`

2. Copy only the number that appears after `/company/`.

   In the example above, the number is: `105616287`

3. Enter that number in your issuer account settings on That's Me, under the **Settings** tab.

With that, issued certificates will display your organization as the issuer on LinkedIn.
