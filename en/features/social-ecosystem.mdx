That's Me isn't just a certificate repository. It's a social ecosystem where achievements drive visibility, connections, and new opportunities.

## Social feed

The platform's home page displays public achievements from the community across two tabs:

| Tab | What it shows |
|-----|--------------|
| **Following** | Achievements from people and issuers you follow |
| **Explore** | Public and featured achievements from the entire community |

Each post in the feed is displayed as an achievement card with:
- Recipient avatar and name
- 3D trophy image
- Event title and issuer name
- KYB verification badge, when the issuer is verified
- Likes, comments, and share button

<Note>
Only public achievements appear in the feed. Private achievements are visible only to the owner.
</Note>

## Interactions

### Celebrate
Click the **Celebrate** button to interact with an achievement. The first like triggers a confetti animation.

### Comments
Comment on any public achievement. The system supports:
- Threaded replies
- Mentions with `@`, with user autocomplete
- Liking individual comments
- Editing, deleting, or hiding your comments

### Share
Share any achievement via LinkedIn, WhatsApp, X, Facebook, email, or direct link.

## Connections

The connection system works through followers:
- **Follow** profiles of people and issuers
- **Unfollow** at any time
- **Remove followers** from your profile
- **Follow back** people who already follow you

Achievements from people you follow appear in the **Following** tab of the feed.

## Search

Find people and events with the search tool, using a minimum of 2 characters:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Profiles">
    Search by username, name, or company. Results show avatar, name, and profile type (Issuer or Member).
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Events">
    Search by event name. Results show name and location (city/state or Online).
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

Search keeps a **recent history** for quick access to previously visited profiles and events.

## Recommendations

The sidebar on desktop, or the search area on mobile, displays personalized suggestions:

### People to follow
Suggested profiles based on your activity and connections.

### Recommended events
Up to 5 AI-recommended events, split into:
- **Personalized recommendations** — based on your achievement history
- **New events** — to expand discovery of opportunities aligned with your objectives

Each event suggestion shows the event image, name, category, location, date, and a feedback button to refine future recommendations.

## Notifications

The notification bell gathers all interactions related to your account:

| Type | Description |
|------|-------------|
| **Achievement received** | Acceptance request with Accept/Decline buttons |
| **Like** | Someone celebrated your achievement |
| **Comment** | Someone commented on an achievement |
| **Follower** | Someone started following you |
| **Follow request** | Request to follow private profiles, with Accept/Decline options |
| **Next step** | Recommended event available, with **View event** button |
| **System notifications** | Important notices and platform updates |

Notifications are private and visible only to you.

## Privacy

You have control over the visibility of your profile and your achievements:

- **Public or private profile** — in **Settings > Privacy**
- **Public or private achievement** — individually, from each card's menu

<Info>
Even with a private profile, you can share specific achievements via direct link. The public certificate link continues to work.
</Info>
