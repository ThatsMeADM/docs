## Receiving achievements

When an issuer recognizes your effort in an event, you receive a notification via **email** and, when available, via **WhatsApp**. The notification includes the issuer's name, the event, the reason for recognition, and a preview of the 3D trophy.

<Steps>
  <Step title="You receive the notification">
    An email (and optionally WhatsApp) arrives with a direct link to accept the achievement. The notification also appears in the bell icon inside the platform.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create your account or sign in">
    If you don't have an account yet, you'll be invited to create one. The link auto-fills your email.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Accept or decline">
    Review the achievement details and choose **Accept** or **Decline**. The decision is yours.
  </Step>
  <Step title="The achievement goes to your profile">
    When you accept, the certificate is permanently created and appears on your [Achievement Wall](/en/features/achievement-wall) and, if public, in the social feed.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
The acceptance link only works with the email used during issuance. If the issuer used a different email than the one on your account, create your account with the correct email.
</Warning>

### After accepting

You automatically receive a follow-up email sequence:

1. **Congratulations** (1 hour later) — confirmation with a link to add it to LinkedIn
2. **Share reminder** (48 hours) — encouragement to share the achievement
3. **Your path** (7 days) — recommended next events based on your goals

## Viewing achievements

The achievement appears as a post on your wall and in the social feed. When you click it, you see the full certificate with:

- **Recipient name** (with profile link)
- **Issuer name** (with link and verification badge, if KYB-verified)
- **Event name and category**
- **3D digital trophy** — button for interactive fullscreen viewing
- **Reason for recognition** — expandable text
- **Date and location** of issuance
- **Validity** — permanent or with expiration date

Each certificate has a **dedicated public page** at `thatsme.com.br/c/[id]`, which additionally includes:

- Authenticity section with SHA-256 hash and QR code
- Detailed event information
- Recommended next step (if the event has connections)
- List of other recipients from the same event

<Info>
The certificate page is public and accessible without login. Anyone can verify authenticity via the hash or QR code.
</Info>

## Sharing achievements

Use the platform's native buttons to share any achievement in one click:

| Channel | How it works |
|---------|-------------|
| **LinkedIn** | Auto-fills the certification form on your profile |
| **WhatsApp** | Sends a direct message with the certificate link |
| **X (Twitter)** | Opens the tweet composer with the link |
| **Facebook** | Opens the share dialog |
| **Email** | Opens your email client with pre-filled subject and link |
| **Copy link** | Copies the public certificate URL to clipboard |

Every share is tracked in the issuer's engagement metrics.

<Tip>
Every achievement is a story. When you share, you amplify the reach of recognition and inspire others to celebrate their journeys.
</Tip>

## Celebrating achievements

On the certificate page, any visitor can click the **Celebrate** button to send a confetti animation and register a like. Celebrations are counted and displayed on the achievement card.

## Community credibility

The platform uses both automatic and manual processes to ensure authenticity. If something looks off, you can **report** it directly from the achievement interface.

Every achievement is unique, traceable, and protected by a SHA-256 hash — ensuring it is legitimate and tamper-proof.
