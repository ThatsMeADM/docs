## That's Me pioneering

That's Me is the platform that connects achievements from different contexts to LinkedIn, allowing you to turn recognitions from events, competitions, training programs, and other experiences into verifiable digital certificates.

With this integration, participants from fields like sports, education, corporate, and sociocultural initiatives can add these achievements to their professional profile with more context and credibility.

## How it works

When you open a received achievement, you'll see the blue **"In"** button in the bottom-right corner.

When you click that button, you'll be directed to the LinkedIn **"Add license or certificate"** screen, with the fields already filled in, such as:

- Achievement name
- Issuing organization
- Issue date
- Verification code and link

Then, just click **Save** to add the achievement to your LinkedIn profile as a certificate.
