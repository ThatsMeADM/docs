## A That's Me exclusive

That's Me is the **only platform in the world that connects events outside the corporate universe to LinkedIn**, turning achievements in tournaments, championships, and other recognitions into **official, verifiable certificates**. This pioneering integration lets participants from any field — sports, education, corporate, or social — strengthen their professional profiles with recognized trophies.

## How it works

When you open a received achievement, you'll see the blue **"In - Add to Profile"** button.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click the LinkedIn button">
    On the achievement page, click the blue **"In - Add to Profile"** button in the bottom-right corner.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review the pre-filled form">
    LinkedIn's **"Add license or certificate"** screen opens with all fields already filled:
    - Achievement name (e.g., "International Marathon")
    - Issuing organization (e.g., **Dream Factory**)
    - Issue date
    - Verification code and authenticated link
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Click **Save** — your achievement now appears on your LinkedIn profile as an official certificate.
  </Step>
</Steps>
