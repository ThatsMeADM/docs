## Platform fundamentals

- **Platform** — Digital service that centralizes the creation, issuance, viewing, and sharing of achievements.
- **Achievement** — A record of recognition, such as a victory, merit, participation, or goal reached, issued by an **Issuer**. In practice, an achievement can be presented as a **digital certificate**, **3D badge**, **3D trophy**, or **TM**, depending on the platform context.
- **Digital achievement** — The digital form of representing an achievement issued on the platform. May include a certificate, public page, 3D badge, 3D trophy, and sharing features.
- **Recognition** — The act of formally acknowledging someone for an accomplishment. On That's Me, this recognition is turned into an **Achievement**, accompanied by a **Digital Certificate** and a **3D TM** visual representation.
- **Digital Certificate** — A verifiable digital record of an achievement. On the platform, this term can refer to both the certificate itself and its dedicated public page, which includes event data, issuer details, reason, and 3D visual elements.
- **3D Badge** — A three-dimensional visual representation of an achievement. In many platform contexts, it functions as a synonym for **TM**, **3D trophy**, or the visual element of the **digital certificate**.
- **3D Trophy** — A three-dimensional visual object used to represent an achievement. In product contexts, it may appear as a synonym for **TM**, **3D badge**, or the visual representation of the achievement.
- **3D TM** — Shorthand for the **Trophy Model**, the **3D badge**, or the **3D trophy** linked to an achievement.
- **Digital Experience** — The set of user interactions with an achievement: 3D viewing in the browser, public page, social feed, and sharing metadata.
- **Validation** — The technical and operational process that reinforces the authenticity of an achievement, using issuer verification, logs, unique IDs, and anti-fraud checks.
- **Achievement ID** — A unique, traceable identifier used for verification and auditing.
- **Unique link (public/verifiable)** — The Achievement Page URL with social metadata, used for public viewing and sharing.

## Users

- **Issuer** — A person or organization that creates **Events**, selects **TMs**, and issues **Achievements**.
- **Administrator** — A member with management permissions over events, issuances, and TMs. All relevant actions are logged for auditing.
- **Member / Regular user** — A person who receives, organizes, and shares achievements on their **Achievement Wall**.

---

## Recognition objects and content

- **TM (Trophy Model)** — A 3D visual model that represents an achievement. In practice, it can be referred to as a **3D trophy**, **3D badge**, or the main visual element of the digital certificate.
- **Trophy Model** — The full name of **TM**. It is the 3D model used to visually represent an achievement on the platform.
- **Essential TM** — A TM configurable through a self-service flow, with adjustments such as colors, shapes, icons, and text.
- **Exclusive TM** — A custom-made TM created from a brief, with a dedicated production and review flow.
- **Import Model** — A function to upload your own 3D model, typically as a `.zip` package containing `.glb`, `.fbx`, and texture files.
- **Configure TM** — Customization of the model's appearance, such as materials, colors, and effects, when available in the chosen flow.
- **Hero's Journey** — Text written by the issuer to explain the reason or context behind the recognition.

## Flows and operations

- **Event** — A structure created by the issuer to organize an issuance. It groups together name, category, dates, location, TMs, and issued certificates.
- **TMs & Issuances (Event)** — The screen where the issuer links TMs to the event and starts issuing achievements.
- **Issuance** — The process of generating digital certificates for recipients. Includes reason, awardees, review, and delivery.
- **Achievement Reason** — A descriptive field used to record the reason for the recognition, such as placement, performance, participation, or contribution.
- **CSV Import** — Bulk addition of awardees via a `.csv` file. Each email corresponds to 1 issuance.
- **Issuance Panel** — The list of issuances for an event, showing the TM used, reason, email, and certificate status.
- **Proof Ready** — A stage in the TM creation flow indicating that a version is available for approval.
- **Finalize Event** — An action that closes the event to further edits and new issuances.

## 3D viewing

- **3D** — A three-dimensional representation displayed digitally in the browser.
- **3D Viewing (WebGL)** — Interactive rendering of the TM, 3D trophy, or 3D badge in the browser.
- **Full Screen** — A viewing mode that expands the 3D model display for a more immersive experience.

---

## Security, verification, and compliance

- **KYB (Know Your Business)** — A verification process applied to issuers to reinforce the organization's credibility.
- **Verification Seal** — A visual indicator that the issuer has undergone a verification process.
- **Anti-fraud** — A set of automatic and manual checks to detect misuse or inconsistencies.
- **Encryption & Authentication** — Technical protections for data and accounts, including access controls and authentication mechanisms.
- **Action Logs (Admin)** — A traceable record of administrative activities, used for history and auditing.
- **LGPD & Privacy** — A set of data protection practices, visibility controls, and personal information management.

---

## Social and integrations

- **Achievement Wall** — The user's profile with achievements organized by date, featuring filtering, privacy, and sharing capabilities.
- **Social Ecosystem (Feed)** — A real-time feed of public achievements, with interactions such as likes, comments, and sharing.
- **Connections / Followers** — Relationships between members and issuers to follow activities and prioritize content in the feed.
- **Notifications** — A centralized area for interactions, new achievements, followers, and relevant updates.
- **Share** — Native sharing features for LinkedIn, WhatsApp, X, email, and link copying.
- **Social Metadata** — Information such as `og:title`, `og:description`, and a featured image, used to generate previews when sharing links.
- **LinkedIn Integration** — Auto-fill of LinkedIn's "Licenses and Certificates" form with achievement data.
- **Achievement Page** — A public page dedicated to an achievement, with the digital certificate, event context, and 3D model viewing.

---

## Store, assets, and plans

- **TM Creator** — The editor used to create or customize TMs, 3D badges, and 3D trophies.
- **My Orders** — The area for tracking TM projects currently in production.
- **TM Gallery** — The issuer's repository for viewing, organizing, renaming, and describing previously created TMs.
- **Plan / Subscription** — A monthly or annual billing model with an included issuance quota.
- **Monthly quota** — The number of issuances included in the subscribed plan.
- **Overage** — Issuances made beyond the monthly quota, charged per unit on the next invoice.

---

## Metrics, strategy, and value terms

- **Engagement** — Interactions generated by achievements, such as likes, shares, reach, and participation in new journeys.
- **Micro-wins** — Partial results recognized to reinforce motivation and continuity.
- **Employer Branding** — Strengthening the employer brand through shared and verifiable achievements.
- **Gamification** — The use of challenges, goals, and digital rewards to encourage participation and retention.

---

## Technologies and files

- **WebGL** — A web standard used for 3D graphics rendering in the browser.
- **.glb / .fbx / textures** — Formats used for importing 3D models and their materials.
- **Open Graph / Twitter Cards** — Metadata standards used to generate previews on social networks.

---

## Community trust actions

- **Report** — An action used to flag suspicions or inconsistencies in an achievement.
- **Privacy (profile/achievement)** — Public or private visibility control applied to the profile and to individual achievements.
