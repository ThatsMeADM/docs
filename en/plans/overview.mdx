## Plans at a glance

| Plan | Issuances/mo | Price (BRL) | Price (USD) | API tier |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Free** | 0 | R\$ 0 | \$0 | — |
| **Starter** | 200 | R\$ 149/mo | \$29/mo | Basic |
| **Pro** | 1,000 | R\$ 749/mo | \$129/mo | Advanced |
| **Business** | 5,000 | R\$ 2,499/mo | \$449/mo | Advanced |
| **Enterprise** | Negotiated | Custom | Custom | Advanced |

Annual billing gives you **2 months free** (17% discount). The amount is charged as a single annual payment.

## What is an issuance?

An **issuance** is a single certificate emitted to a recipient. Each issuance:

- Creates a unique SHA-256 hash for verification
- Delivers the certificate via email (and WhatsApp, if enabled)
- Counts toward your monthly quota
- Is **permanent** — it never expires, even if you cancel your plan

One event with 50 recipients = 50 issuances.

## Billing cycle

Your quota resets on your **billing cycle date** — the day you subscribed — not on the 1st of every month.

Example: if you subscribed on March 15th, your quota resets on the 15th of each month.

<Tip>
  You can check your current usage and billing cycle date at **Settings → Billing**.
</Tip>

## What happens when you cancel

- **Existing certificates** remain active and verifiable forever
- **New issuances** are blocked immediately
- **Events** remain visible in the public directory for 90 days (grace period)
- Your account reverts to the Free plan
