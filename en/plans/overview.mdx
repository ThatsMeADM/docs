## Plans

| Plan | Issuances/month | Price (BRL) | Price (USD) | API Tier |
|---|---:|---|---|---|
| **Free** | 0 | R\$0 | \$0 | — |
| **Starter** | 200 | R\$149/mo | \$29/mo | Basic |
| **Pro** | 1,000 | R\$749/mo | \$129/mo | Advanced |
| **Business** | 5,000 | R\$2,499/mo | \$449/mo | Advanced |
| **Enterprise** | Negotiated | Custom | Custom | Advanced |

The annual plan gives you **2 months free**, equivalent to a 17% discount. The amount is charged as a single annual payment.

## What is an issuance?

An **issuance** is a certificate sent to a recipient. Each issuance:

- Generates a unique SHA-256 hash for verification
- Delivers the certificate by email and, when enabled, by WhatsApp
- Counts toward your monthly limit
- Remains active after issuance

An event with 50 recipients consumes 50 issuances.

## Billing cycle

Your quota renews on your **billing cycle date** — that is, the day your subscription started, not the first day of each month.

Example: if you subscribed on March 15, your quota renews on the 15th of every month.

<Tip>
Check your current usage and billing cycle date at **Settings → Billing**.
</Tip>

## What happens when you cancel

- **Already-issued certificates** remain active and verifiable
- **New issuances** are blocked immediately
- **Events** remain visible in the public directory for 30 days
- Your account reverts to the Free plan
