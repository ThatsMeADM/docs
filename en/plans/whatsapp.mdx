## Why WhatsApp?

WhatsApp is the primary delivery channel for certificates on That's Me. Compared to email:

| Channel | Open rate | Avg. time to open |
|---|---|---|
| Email | ~20% | Hours to days |
| WhatsApp | ~90% | Minutes |

Recipients receive a rich message with a link to view, accept, and share their certificate.

## How it works by plan

### Starter — add-on

WhatsApp is an **optional add-on** on the Starter plan:
- Cost: **+R$ 49/month** (+$9/month)
- Activate at **Settings → Billing → Add-ons**
- When active, all issuances include WhatsApp delivery
- When inactive, certificates are delivered by email only

### Pro, Business, Enterprise — included

WhatsApp delivery is **included in all issuances** at no extra cost. This applies to both within-quota and overage issuances.

## Message type

All certificate delivery messages are classified as **utility messages** under Meta's WhatsApp Business API. This means:

- They are transactional notifications (not marketing)
- They comply with WhatsApp's commerce policy
- Recipients can opt out of future messages

<Tip>
  That's Me never sends marketing or promotional messages via WhatsApp.
  Every message is a direct response to a certificate being issued.
</Tip>

## Supported countries

WhatsApp delivery works in all countries where the WhatsApp Business API is available. The message cost is a passthrough from Meta's pricing — it's already included in your plan or overage pricing.

## Activating and deactivating

**Starter plan:**
1. Go to **Settings → Billing → Add-ons**
2. Click **Activate WhatsApp** (or Deactivate)
3. Changes take effect immediately

**Pro and above:** WhatsApp is always active and cannot be deactivated.

## What recipients see

Recipients receive a WhatsApp message containing:
- The issuer's name and event name
- A direct link to view and accept the certificate
- A preview image of the 3D badge

The message is sent from That's Me's verified WhatsApp Business number.
