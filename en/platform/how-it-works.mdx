---
title: "How It Works"
description: "The full flow from event creation to profile display"
---

<Steps>
  <Step title="Issuer creates an Event">
    Define the name, category, location, and dates.
    The AI automatically extracts skills, level, and keywords from the description.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Issuer creates a Badge">
    Design a 3D badge in the editor or use a template.
    Upload your logo — the badge is ready in seconds.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Issuer emits certificates">
    Upload a list of recipients (name + email + optional phone).
    Each recipient receives an email (and optionally WhatsApp) with their certificate.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Recipient accepts the certificate">
    Recipient clicks the link, creates an account, and the certificate
    appears on their public profile automatically.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Platform recommends the next step">
    Based on the recipient's goals and certificate history,
    That's Me recommends the next relevant event — driving upsell for the issuer.
  </Step>
</Steps>
