The Badge Creator is where you design the 3D digital trophies that will be used as recognition in your events.

<Tip>
TMs are **free on every plan**. Create as many as you want at no additional cost.
</Tip>

## Three creation paths

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Essential TM">
    ### Self-service editor

    The 3D editor lets you create trophies in minutes with real-time preview:

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Upload an image">
        Upload a 2D image (JPG, SVG, or PDF). The editor automatically converts it into a 3D model.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Customize">
        Adjust in the editor:
        - **Geometry** — extrusion depth, bevel
        - **Materials** — realistic (PBR), glass, or prism
        - **Textures** — 15+ presets (metal, leather, marble, holographic...)
        - **Colors** — solid or gradient
        - **Animations** — rotation, bounce, breath
        - **Circular text** — with independent font and material
        - **Lighting** — HDR environments, bloom/neon
      </Step>
      <Step title="Publish">
        Approve the result and publish. Delivery within **2 business days**.
      </Step>
    </Steps>

    <Info>
    The editor is self-service — you have full control over the final result without depending on a designer.
    </Info>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Exclusive TM">
    ### Custom design

    For trophies built from scratch by the design team. The brief is guided in 4 steps:

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Style">
        Define adjectives (modern, elegant, energetic) and visual structure (geometric, organic, emblematic).
      </Step>
      <Step title="Materials and effects">
        Choose detail level, main materials (noble metal, crystal, marble), motion effects, and internal lighting.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Details">
        TM name, purpose, target audience, notes, and design references.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Confirmation">
        Review the complete summary and confirm. The team begins production.
      </Step>
    </Steps>

    - First version within **5 business days**
    - **2 revision rounds** included
    - Dedicated contact with the team
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Import Model">
    ### Bring your own 3D

    If you already have a model ready:

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Upload files">
        Upload a .ZIP with .GLB, .FBX, and textures. Optional: up to 3 reference images.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Details">
        Name and description for internal organization.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Confirmation">
        Review and confirm. Integration time: **2-3 business days**.
      </Step>
    </Steps>

    <Warning>
    Technical requirements: functional file, corrected normals, applied textures, defined colors. The team does not edit the original design.
    </Warning>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Drafts

The editor automatically saves your work (auto-save with 800ms debounce). Drafts remain available for **24 hours** and are listed on the editor page.

## My Orders

Track TM production in **My Orders**:

- **In progress** — detailed status: Order Received → In Production → Proof Ready → Completed
- **Completed** — history of finished orders

## Gallery approval

After completion, the TM appears in the [Badge Gallery](/en/for-issuers/badge-gallery) as pending. Approval is done in the gallery — only after acceptance is the TM available for issuance.

<Warning>
Only TMs with **Accepted** status in the Gallery are available for use in events.
</Warning>

## Export formats

Each published TM is automatically exported in:

| Format | Use |
|--------|-----|
| **GLB** | Complete 3D model (embedded materials, textures, animations) |
| **USDZ** | Augmented reality on iPhone and Mac |
| **Video** | MP4/WebM for social media |
| **Thumbnail** | Static PNG for previews |

For full technical editor details, see [3D Trophies](/en/introduction/3d-trophies).
