The Administrators section lets you grant access to your issuer account to other people in your organization.

## What administrators can do

Administrators have **full access** to the issuer dashboard:

- Create and manage events
- Issue achievements
- Acquire and manage TMs
- Track issuance status

<Warning>
Inviting someone as an administrator grants full access to the issuer account, **except** for profile settings and billing data.
</Warning>

## Manage administrators

Access via account menu → **Administrators**. The screen shows the full list with:

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| ID | Unique identifier |
| Email | Registered email |
| Name | Full name |
| Nickname | Platform username |
| Actions | Remove menu |

Use the search field at the top to filter by email or name.

### Add

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click '+ Add Team'">
    In the top-right corner of the screen.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter the email">
    Type the new administrator's email address.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm">
    An invitation is sent automatically via email. Once accepted, the administrator gains full access.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Remove

1. Find the administrator in the table
2. Click the **"..."** menu on their row
3. Select **"Delete"**

## Security and traceability

All actions performed by administrators are **recorded in internal logs**:

- Issuances executed
- Event changes
- Emission movements
- Any relevant modifications

<Note>
These records are auditable and can be reviewed at any time by the support team or the primary issuer.
</Note>
