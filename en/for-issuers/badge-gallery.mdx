The TM Gallery is where you manage all the 3D trophies on your issuer account. Here you can view, organize, and edit collection information, as well as easily obtain the badgeID.

<Info>
All TMs are linked to the issuer account and can be used and reused across multiple events, as many times as needed, by any authorized administrator on the team.
</Info>

## Features

### Viewing and organization

- **TM grid** — all models you have created, with search and sorting
- **Search** — find TMs by name, description, or ID
- **Sort** — newest, oldest, name A–Z or Z–A
- **Modal** — immersive 3D trophy viewing in the browser, with options to export video and display in fullscreen
- **Copy ID** — copy the TM identifier for API usage

### Metadata editing

Each TM's **name** and **description** can be edited directly in the gallery. This is especially useful for teams with multiple administrators, as it makes it easier to identify and correctly use TMs across different events.

<Tip>
Use standardized names and clear descriptions for your TMs to streamline your team's work, especially during events with multiple simultaneous issuances. Only the account's administrators have access to this information.
</Tip>

### Deletion

TMs can only be deleted if they have **no linked issuances**. After the first issuance, the TM becomes permanently associated with the generated certificates.
