## How emissions work

Each certificate sent to a recipient counts as **1 emission**. Your monthly quota depends on your plan:

| Plan | Emissions/month | Monthly price |
|------|----------------|---------------|
| **Free** | 0 | $0 |
| **Starter** | 200 | $29 |
| **Pro** | 1,000 | $129 |
| **Business** | 5,000 | $449 |
| **Enterprise** | Negotiated | Custom |

An event with 50 recipients = 50 emissions consumed from the quota.

## Billing cycle

Your quota renews on your **cycle date** — the day you subscribed, not the 1st of the month.

Check your current usage in **Settings → Billing**.

## Overage

Overage allows you to keep issuing after the monthly quota is reached. Each extra emission is charged per unit on the next invoice.

| Plan | Without WhatsApp | With WhatsApp |
|------|-----------------|---------------|
| **Starter** | $0.05/emission | $0.08/emission |
| **Pro** | — | $0.08/emission |
| **Business** | — | $0.07/emission |

You can:
- **Enable/disable** overage at any time
- **Set a monthly spending cap** for overage

Access in **Settings → Billing → Overage**. For full details, see [Overage](/en/plans/overage).

<Warning>
Overage is enabled by default on paid plans. If you don't want to exceed the quota, disable it or set a limit.
</Warning>

## TMs are free

TMs (Trophy Models) are **free on every plan**. Create, customize, or import as many 3D models as you want at no additional cost. Billing applies only to certificate emissions.

## Annual plan

The annual plan offers **2 months free** (17% discount). The amount is charged as a single annual payment.
