## How emissions work

Each certificate sent to a recipient counts as **1 emission**. The monthly quota available depends on your plan:

| Plan | Emissions/month | Monthly price |
|------|----------------|---------------|
| **Free** | 0 | R$ 0 |
| **Starter** | 200 | R$ 149 |
| **Pro** | 1,000 | R$ 749 |
| **Business** | 5,000 | R$ 2,499 |
| **Enterprise** | 15,000+ | Contact us |

An issuance to 50 recipients consumes 50 emissions from the quota.

## Billing cycle

Your quota renews on your **cycle date** — the day your subscription started, not the 1st of the month.

You can track your current usage in **Settings → Billing**.

## Overage

Overage allows you to keep issuing after the monthly quota is reached. Each extra emission is charged per unit on the next invoice.

| Plan | Without WhatsApp | With WhatsApp |
|------|-----------------|---------------|
| **Starter** | R$ 0.25 per emission | R$ 0.40 per emission |
| **Pro** | — | R$ 0.40 per emission |
| **Business** | — | R$ 0.35 per emission |

You can:

- **Enable or disable** overage at any time
- **Set a monthly spending cap** for overage

Access the setting in **Settings → Billing → Overage**. For full details, see [Overage](/en/plans/overage).

<Warning>
Overage is enabled by default on paid plans. If you do not want to exceed the quota, disable this option or set a spending limit.
</Warning>

## TMs are free

TMs (3D Trophies) are **free on every plan**. You can create and customize as many models as you want in the editor at no additional cost. Billing applies only to certificate emissions.

<Note>
Events created by issuers on the Free plan remain on the platform and can be found by users, but they do not appear in the public directory and are not distributed by the platform's AI.
</Note>

## Annual plan

The annual plan offers **2 months free**, equivalent to a discount of approximately 17%. The amount is charged as a single annual payment.

<Info>
To compare plans, features, and pricing in more detail, see [Plans Overview](/en/plans/overview).
</Info>
