---
title: "Issuer Quickstart"
description: "Create an event and issue your first certificate in 5 minutes"
---

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create your account">
    Create an account at app.thatsme.com.br as an **Issuer** (Emissor).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create a Badge">
    Go to **Gallery → New Badge**. Upload your logo or use a template.
    Your 3D badge is ready in under a minute.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create an Event">
    Go to **Events → New Event**. Fill in the name, category, and description.
    The AI will analyze your event and extract skills automatically.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Issue certificates">
    Open the event, click **Issue**, select your badge, and add recipients
    (name + email). Click **Confirm** — emails are sent immediately.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Track results">
    Go to **Dashboard** to see adoption rate, trail conversions, and
    how many recipients advanced to the next course.
  </Step>
</Steps>
