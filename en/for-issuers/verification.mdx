That's Me was designed as a professional and trustworthy recognition platform. Every issuance carries context, authorship, date, and technical validation.

## Certificate integrity

Every certificate receives a **SHA-256 hash** generated at the time of issuance. The hash is computed from:

```
post_id | event_id | badge_id | profile_id | user_id | created_at | kyb_verification_id
```

Anyone can verify authenticity at:

```
https://thatsme.com.br/certificate/verify/[hash]
```

Verification confirms:
- The certificate was issued by That's Me
- The data has not been tampered with
- Whether the issuer was verified (KYB) at the time of issuance

<Info>
The hash is **immutable**. Even if an issuer loses verification later, certificates issued during the verified period retain the verification ID in the hash — permanent proof.
</Info>

## KYB — Know Your Business

Organizations can apply for verification in **Settings → Verification**. Two paths available:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Domain verification">
    ### Status: BASIC

    Add a TXT record to your domain's DNS. Verification is **immediate** and confirms the organization controls the stated domain.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Document upload">
    ### Status: VERIFIED

    Submit the company's official registration document. Review in **1 to 3 business days**.

    VERIFIED status displays a **verification badge** on the issuer's profile and on every certificate they issue.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

| Status | What it means |
|--------|--------------|
| **BASIC** | Domain control confirmed |
| **VERIFIED** | Verification badge visible on profile and all certificates |

## Full traceability

Every issuance is:

- **Recorded with date and time**, linked to the responsible administrator
- **Auditable**, with a permanent record in the action history
- **Associated with a unique 3D model**, reinforcing the visual identity

<Note>
Every action performed by an administrator (creating events, issuing achievements, adding members) is automatically recorded. This enables internal audits and accountability.
</Note>

## Community moderation

If any achievement appears suspicious or out of context, **any platform member can report it** by clicking **"..."** on the post and selecting **"Report"**.

Reports are reviewed by the team with support from automated checks and pattern analysis. This open audit model strengthens the ecosystem's trust.

## LGPD and data protection

That's Me is fully compliant with Brazil's **General Data Protection Law (LGPD)**:

- No personal information is disclosed without explicit consent
- Achievements only become public if the member opts in
- Users can make their profile private at any time
- Data can be deleted upon request
