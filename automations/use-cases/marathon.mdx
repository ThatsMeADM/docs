## The scenario

A race organizer runs 200+ events per year. After each race, finishers
receive a digital certificate with their finish time and position.
Previously this was manual — now it's automated.

## The setup

**Tools:** Google Sheets (race results) + N8N + That's Me API + Webhooks

**Result:** Certificate issued within 5 minutes of finish time being logged.
The recipient receives an email. When they claim it, the organizer's CRM is updated.

## The flow

```
Race timing system exports results to Google Sheets
    ↓
N8N monitors sheet — new row with status "Official"
    ↓
POST /api/v1.0/invitations
  reason: "Completed 42km in {{ finish_time }} — Position #{{ position }}"
    ↓
Recipient receives certificate email
    ↓
Webhook: certificate.accepted
    ↓
N8N updates CRM record with certificate URL
    ↓
N8N sends WhatsApp message: "Your certificate is ready"
```

## Why it works

The certificate's `reason` field becomes the personal achievement text on the certificate.
Include finish time, category, position — the more specific, the more meaningful.
